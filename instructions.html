Instructions

Single quotes may not appear inside parameters, all other characters are valid.

The Gender, Age, and Ethnicity parameters, when single words, can appear on their own, do not need quotes around them, and are case insensitive. The following are valid entries.

female 39 Chinese

Male 21 white

When the Gender or Ethnicity parameters are multiple words, they should appear in single quotes, the following are valid entries.

'transgender woman' 52 hispanic

male 12 'african american'

Lab information should be stylized "'NameOfLab':'LabValue':'LabUnits':'LabStatement'", where each value is enclosed in single quotes.

The fourth parameter is optional. The folowing are some valid examples of lab info.

'Hermatocrit':'42':'%'

'HDL':'67':'mg/dL':'Up from last visit.'

Medications should be stylized "'MedName':'MedStrength':'MedDoseage':'MedRoute':'MedFrequency':'SelectionCriteria':'BeforeOrAfterVisit'.

Any irrelevant fields may be left blank for medications. The 'BeforeOrAfterVisit' parameter indicates whether the patient was taking the medication before the visit or will be taking it after the visit. Leave blank if they were taking it before and will continue taking it after the visit. The following examples are all valid.

'Methylphenidate':'18 mg ER':'18 mg':'oral':'Take 1 in the morning':'Switched to ER as patient says they are too antsy in the morning':'after'

'Clonazepam':'2 mg':'2 mg':'':'Take half a tablet in the morning, and half at night':'':''

'Fluvoxamine':'200 mg':'100 mg':'':'':'after'
