Enter Keywords

Enter keywords relating to the case. Disease state information is the most important. Lab values should be stylized "NameOfLab:LabValue:LabUnits" all without whitespace. The third parameter "LabUnits" is not necessary for labs which measure percentages or other self-explanatory units, but may still be used. For example, "Hermatocrit:42%", "Hermatocrit:42:%", and "HDL:67:mg/dL" are all valid, but "HDL:67mg/dL" is not. Numbers which appear on their own are assumed to be ages.
